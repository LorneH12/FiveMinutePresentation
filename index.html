Why No-Bake?

  • Fool-proof texture‚Äîno cracking, no water bath.

Ingredients (Base Recipe)

Crust

  • 200 g graham cracker crumbs
  • 80 g unsalted butter, melted
  • 20 g sugar (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

  • 500 g cream cheese, softened
  • 120 g powdered sugar
  • 240 ml heavy cream (cold)
  • 10 ml vanilla extract
  • 1 lemon: zest + 10 ml juice

Make the Crust

  1. Combine crumbs, sugar, salt.

  1. Beat cream cheese + powdered sugar until smooth.
  2. Whip cold cream to soft peaks; fold into cheese.
  3. Add vanilla, lemon zest/juice; fold until silky.
  4. Spoon into crust, level the top.
